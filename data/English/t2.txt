Small is a Distinguished Fellow of the Institution of Professional Engineers New Zealand and was the group's president in 1996-97. In 2013, he received the individual award from the Railway Technical Society of Australasia for outstanding service to New Zealand's rail industry. In 2015, he funded the Francis Small Scholarship at the University of Auckland, available to civil, electrical, mechanical or chemical engineering students.

In 1999, Small was awarded the 278th Bronze Wolf, the only distinction of the World Organization of the Scout Movement, awarded by the World Scout Committee for exceptional services to world scouting.
